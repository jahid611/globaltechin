Service Overview

GlobalTechin's Precision Machining services deliver exceptional quality and accuracy for even the most complex components. Our advanced CNC technology and skilled technicians ensure that every part meets the most stringent specifications and tolerances required by our clients.

Whether you need prototype development, small batch production, or large-scale manufacturing, our comprehensive machining capabilities can accommodate your requirements. We specialize in producing high-precision components for critical applications across various industries, including oil and gas, petrochemical, nuclear, and renewable energy sectors.

Our Capabilities

GlobalTechin's machining capabilities utilize conventional and CNC machines. We offer capacities up to 2000 mm diameter, 6000mm length and 10T weight per piece.

With the latest CNC technology, we can manufacture the most complex components for all industries and in accordance with our clients' demands. The most stringent tolerances can be validated by using tridimensional and profile control.
