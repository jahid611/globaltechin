Heat Treatment Solutions

We offer a comprehensive range of heat treatment processes to enhance material properties and meet specific performance requirements. Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced team ensure consistent quality and reliable results for every project.

Annealing

Controlled heating and cooling to reduce hardness, increase ductility, and relieve internal stresses

Normalizing

Heating above critical temperature followed by air cooling to refine grain structure and improve mechanical properties

Quenching

Rapid cooling from high temperature to increase hardness and strength through controlled phase transformation

Tempering

Controlled reheating after quenching to achieve optimal balance between hardness and ductility

Solution Treatment

Heating and rapid cooling to dissolve precipitates and create a supersaturated solid solution

Precipitation Hardening

Controlled aging to form fine precipitates that strengthen the material through precipitation mechanisms
