Service Overview

GlobalTechin offers comprehensive fabrication and coating services, providing efficient and cost-effective solutions for a wide range of components. Our expertise spans from welded structures and assemblies for wellhead equipment to pressure vessels, spools, and skids for onshore, offshore, and nuclear markets.

Our pressure vessels are designed using the latest software available on the market according to ASME code by a team of highly specialized engineers. These products are used in major specialized projects worldwide and are fabricated in a variety of materials to meet our customers' specific requirements.
