HSE & Quality

Our commitment to health, safety, and quality excellence is at the core of everything we do at GlobalTechin. We believe that maintaining the highest standards is essential for our success and the satisfaction of our clients.

Safety is our top priority

GLOBALTECHIN – Safety comes first

GlobalTechin is totally committed to the protection of its employees, customers and contractors from any harm or loss resulting from the company's activities. Globally we strive for continual improvement in all areas of health and safety.

We pursue these improvements through compliance with all applicable legal requirements, providing and maintaining a safe and healthy working environment and working together with our employees, clients and visitors to achieve our goal of Zero Accidents by communicating and applying our HSE policy statement.

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

At GlobalTechin, we enforce strict safety protocols including mandatory use of appropriate personal protective equipment. Our comprehensive safety program ensures that all personnel are properly equipped with the necessary safety gear for their specific tasks.

Hard Hat

Mandatory head protection in all operational areas to prevent head injuries from falling objects and impacts.

Safety Glasses

Eye protection against flying particles, dust, and chemical splashes during manufacturing operations.

Protective Gloves

Hand protection against cuts, abrasions, chemicals, and thermal hazards during material handling.

Construction Vest

High-visibility clothing to ensure workers are easily seen in all operational environments.

Protective Boots

Safety footwear with steel toes and anti-slip soles to protect against falling objects and slippery surfaces.

Safety Awareness

Proper signage and safety markers to identify hazardous areas and maintain safe working conditions.
